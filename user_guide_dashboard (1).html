User Guide: Interactive Thesis Dashboard

This dashboard is designed to support the thesis on Kansei Engineering and Social Media-Based Stocktaking for reusable plastic bottles. It consists of four interactive modules that visualize emotional clustering, demand forecasting, product design feedback, and social media sentiment analysis.

1. Kansei Emotion Cluster Viewer

Visualize emotional profiles based on Kansei words.

Select a Kansei word from the dropdown. The radar chart will update to show how that emotion scores across five dimensions: Softness, Sustainability, Innovation, Trust, and Design.

Helps identify emotional clusters that influence product perception.

2. Demand Forecast Chart

Compare actual and forecasted demand over 12 months.

View the line chart showing two datasets: Actual Demand and Forecasted Demand. This helps visualize trends and prediction accuracy.

Demonstrates the effectiveness of time series forecasting (e.g., XGBoost) in stocktaking.

3. Product Design Feedback

Provide design suggestions based on selected emotions.

Choose an emotion from the dropdown. The feedback box will display recommended design features (e.g., color, shape, texture).

Links emotional preferences to actionable product design strategies.

4. Social Media Sentiment Explorer

Analyze sentiment and keyword trends from social media hashtags.

Select a hashtag from the dropdown. The bar chart shows sentiment distribution (Positive, Neutral, Negative), and the word cloud displays frequently used terms.

Validates consumer sentiment and trends using social media data.

This dashboard is fully browser-based and requires no installation. It can be hosted online or embedded in presentations.
